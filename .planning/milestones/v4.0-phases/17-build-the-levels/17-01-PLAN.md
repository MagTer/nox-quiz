<objective>
Add per-level camera bounds so later levels can be longer than `CONFIG.LEVEL_RIGHT` without a global camera clamp change. Keep `level-01` behavior byte-for-byte identical by defaulting to the existing `CONFIG.LEVEL_*` constants when `bounds` is omitted.

Purpose: This is the foundation for the length ramp in D-05; without it, the camera on `level-04` would stop at 2240 px and show void beyond the goal.
Output: A backward-compatible `followCamera` and a `game.js` that threads `level.bounds` through it.
</objective>
