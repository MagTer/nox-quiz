## Decisions Made
- Reused the proven 34.6-02/05/06 calm-intro skeleton (mandatory stepped climb+descent over a wide pit + low/high fork) as the gatehouse + battlement-fork template, then ESCALATED to the L7–8 band by putting REAL overlapping-tier ceilings on the REQUIRED grand staircase (not an optional route as levels 03/05 did) — legal because a monotonic open stacked staircase stays legible.
- Made the grand staircase a MONOTONIC rightward climb (no reversal) to hold the LVL-02 L7≠L8 split — level-08 owns the switchback; level-07 owns the clean staircase.
- Chose 6 overlapping tiers at rise 73 to reach a 420px climb (the tallest odd, top of the §8.5 ~320-420 odd band) while keeping every rise inside the 88.331px envelope and every headroom at the 25px safe floor.

## Deviations from Plan
**Deviation 1 [Rule 1 - Bug]: the broken-drawbridge stepping stones as first authored broke spawn->goal reachability.** The first cut used three narrow (w:80) same-height stones at 80px gaps. The reach model uses a FIXED ~162px reach for a flat (dy=0) hop, so a landing platform must CONTAIN takeoffEdge+162 — an 80px-wide stone at an 80px gap gives spanMax 160 < 162, so the hop overshoots and the far floor (+ 11 downstream coins) read as unreachable (validate-levels: 13 HARD-FAIL). Fixed inline by redesigning the drawbridge as a robust 2-stone crossing sized to the model: F5->SS1 is a rise (long root ~132 lands inside SS1), SS1->SS2 is a flat hop onto a 120px-wide stone (fixed 162 lands inside), SS2->F6 is a drop. Re-validated -> zero HARD-FAIL. Found during Task 1; commit `40a0956`. (The PLAN's pre-checkpoint geometry hints were superseded by §8.5 as instructed; the §8.5 raised bar drove the actual shape.)
