# Phase 34.6 Plan 07: Rebuild level-06 (intense/vertical cemetery necropolis, math-skip key) Summary

**Rebuilt `src/levels/level-06.js` from scratch as "The Necropolis" — a ~2.2x-length intense/vertical CEMETERY (goal.x 3300 -> 7200) whose main goal route crests a 666px double-switchback cathedral spire (TALLER than the approved even prototype level-02's 518px), with a diamond fork (two visible routes), a second crypt-tower switchback, two interleaved catacomb descents, and an OPTIONAL math-skip KEY on a 740px above-summit apex (geometry.keys, NO locks) — proven both ways in the real engine.**

| Interleaved DESCENTS | **2** (SU->DA->DB->DC->F5 catacomb stair; SV->VA->F6) |
| Fall-pits (un-jumpable) | **2** (spire-1 pit 2820..4480 =1660px; spire-2 pit 4920..5860 =940px) |

Fall-stakes vs checkpoints (§8.5 rule 4): a missed hop anywhere on either spire drops into a crypt-pit and respawns; the 30-checkpoint cadence (≤700px on every hazard/height stretch, one per climb tier) means the fall costs only seconds and a short re-run — NO game-over, NO timer, NO lost progress. Overlapping-tier ceilings are the L3–6 band (every pair rise 74 -> 26px headroom, ≥70px x-overlap), kept open-air/legible (§8.5 rule 6).
