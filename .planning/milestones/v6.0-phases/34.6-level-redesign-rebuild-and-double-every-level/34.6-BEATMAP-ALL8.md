# Phase 34.6 REDO — Holistic 8-Level Beat-Map (the design contract for the rebuild)

Written 2026-07-16 as the **holistic design pass** the REDO plan §"Build approach" recommends:
all 8 designed as a SET so distinctness is guaranteed cross-level BEFORE any file is built.
Each level gets its own DIRECTION + MACRO-SHAPE + a distinct y-profile character and ground
rhythm, so no two share a layout signature (`check-level-distinctness.mjs`) and none carries a
flat transport run (`check-monotony.mjs`).

six are built fresh to the rows above and must NOT reference each other's structure.

## Shared invariants (every level)

- **Math density LOCKED:** exactly 1 door + 1 enemy + the end gate. Evens (02/04/06/08) ALSO carry
  the math-skip **key** (`geometry.keys`, NO `geometry.locks`) on the hardest branch: key held →
  free clear +20 XP; else the end math gate. Odds carry NO key.
- **Exactly one secret alcove** per level, ~70px above an early/mid platform, off the required path.
- **Coins** ~30–40, each a fly-through box: floor coins at walk height (`FLOOR_Y-56`=264), tier coins
  on each tier's own clear surface; the hardest branch carries the bonus cluster.
- **Physics band:** rise 60–70 (open, no ceiling) / 72–75 (overlapping tiers, headroom 26 with h:16);
  never > 88.331. Climb-tier x-overlap ~70px. Bare gaps ≤160; wider gaps bridged.
- **Checkpoints:** near spawn; before every spike; before every hard climb and every descent; one per
  climb tier. ≤700px spacing on hazard/height stretches.
- **Ramp (§8):** L1–2 open-air, gentle (01 no overlapping tiers). L3–6 ceilings/overlaps appear,
  gaps 140–160. L7–8 tightest. Level 01 stays the MOST forgiving (generous platforms, wide margins,
  dense checkpoints) — forgiving, not flat.
- **Bounds:** explicit 4-field literal; `bounds.right` = final floor end; `bounds.top` a screen above
  the highest entity. Bump by hand when extending.
- **allowedTables:** carry each level's existing pool (swamp 01/02, town 03/04, cemetery 05/06, castle
  07/08 ramp toward 6–9). Kept per existing files; brain.js is LOCKED.

## Per-level skeletons (x grows left→right; y: 320=floor, smaller=higher, negative=above screen top)

### 01 Bog Crossing (lateral archipelago, NO overlapping tiers)
Ground is broken into small islands; the "floor" is mostly a chain of stepping-stone PLATFORMS at
varied heights over water-gaps, never two hops alike. Vertical range ~150 (islands from y254 down to
y186 up to y150). A **fallen-log climb** = 3 rising stones (254→190→150) then a **lily-pad drop** back
down; a **low/high fork** midway (low stones vs a higher coin-bearing arc that rejoins). Door early on
a solid island; enemy past the fork; end gate on the final island before goal. No ceilings (§8 L1).

### 03 Rooftop Descent (town, downward staircase with a mid reclimb)
Spawn HIGH on a rooftop plateau (a raised floor-like platform near y120). Work DOWN through tiered
roofs/awnings (120→190→250→street 320) as drop-ledges. Midway a **chimney reclimb** reverses UP two
tiers (a deliberate ascent inside a descent) then a **market-stall fork** (a low awning route vs a
higher billboard route with the coin cluster) rejoining at street level. Door on the opening rooftop;
enemy at street after the fork. Ends on the street. Ceilings allowed (L3): a couple overlapping awnings.

### 04 Twin Towers (town, up–across–up, key at tower B peak)
**Tower A:** a compact switchback climb off the spawn floor (320→246→172→98→24, ~4 tiers). At A's top a
**high beam traverse** — a run of same-height narrow beams (y~24) spanning a wide pit (the "across"),
each beam a hop. **Descend** the far side of A's traverse via drop-ledges to a mid floor. **Tower B:**
a second switchback climb to a higher peak (up to y-124) carrying the **key** at its summit; the
hardest branch (a narrow high beam) holds the bonus coins. From B's peak, **drop** down drop-ledges to
the goal floor. Door low on the approach to tower A; enemy on the mid floor between the towers.
Distinct from 02/08: two SEPARATE towers with a long high LATERAL traverse between them, not one spire.

### 05 Sunken Vault (cemetery, down–across–up V-valley)
Spawn on a graveyard ledge, **descend INTO a crypt basin**: drop-ledges 320→ a low sunken floor
(the basin is a floor run but reached by dropping past pillars). Cross a **pillared catacomb floor**
— a long low run broken by short pillar-hops (low platforms y~258 as coffin-lid steps) and a spike
cluster to time. Then **climb OUT** the far side via a rising switchback back up to a graveyard exit
ledge and the goal. Fork: a low direct basin-floor route vs a higher ledge route (coins) over the
pillars, rejoining at the out-climb. Door on the descent approach; enemy on the basin floor. The V is
the signature: down, long across, up — opposite of 06's sustained shaft.

### 06 Deep Shaft (cemetery, mostly-down branching descent, key mid-shaft)
Brief **entry climb** off spawn (320→246→172, 2 tiers) to a shaft lip. Then a long **branching DESCENT**
down a necropolis shaft: alternating left/right drop-ledges zig-zagging DOWN (172→24→ -50 → wait, down
means y INCREASES: lip at 172 → 60 no). Correction: descent = y increases toward 320 and the shaft
bottom is a low floor. Ledges step DOWN: 120→180→240→ bottom floor 320, zig-zagging x left/right so
it's a switchback DESCENT (visible doubling-back downward). The **key** sits on a side ledge branching
OFF the main descent (a short up-hop off one landing, off the goal path). Bottom out onto the goal
floor. Door near the shaft lip; enemy on the bottom floor. Distinct: a descending switchback (06) vs
02/08's ascending switchbacks and 03/05's gentler descents — the only mostly-downward INTENSE level.

### 07 The Ramparts (castle, lateral elevated wall-walk)
The whole level runs along an ELEVATED battlement — the "floors" are a chain of wall-top segments at a
raised band (a high floor-like run), with **murder-hole gaps** (short pits between merlons) to hop, a
wide **drawbridge gap** bridged by a mid platform, and a **tower-side fork** (drop to a lower sally-port
walk with coins vs stay high on the parapet). Small parapet steps (rises 40–60, gentle, elevated) — NOT
a monotonic staircase (that was old-07) and NOT a tall climb (that's 08). Vertical range modest but
the whole thing sits high. Door on an early wall segment; enemy past the drawbridge. Ends at a far
tower. Signature: horizontal traverse at altitude with hole-hops — distinct from every other level.

## Build order & validation

Build 01 → 03 → 05 → 07 (odd/calm) then 04 → 06 (even/intense, key). Keep 02, 08 untouched. After
EACH file: `node scripts/validate-levels.mjs && node scripts/check-level-distinctness.mjs && node
scripts/check-monotony.mjs`. After all 8: full suite incl. `browser-boot.mjs`, `audit-coins.mjs`,
`audit-endgate-key.mjs` (evens), `audit-phase21-mechanics.mjs`. Then hand to user for test-play.
