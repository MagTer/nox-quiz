### Deviations during the rework (all auto-fixed, Rule 1/3, caught by actually running it)

1. **[Rule 1] Summit shortcut (goal topped out at 370px, not 518px).** The first v3
   draft let `bottleneckPath` route L2→DA, skipping the summit. **Fix:** widened SU
   rightward so ONLY it reaches descent-1's first ledge DA (verified by re-dumping the
   planned route until it went through SU). Same trick applied to SV/spire-2.
2. **[Rule 3] Mount-edge stalls (`T2→TF`, `L3→SU`).** Upper tiers that extended LEFT of
   the lower tier while the hop was rightward made the rising mount unable to cross the
   leading edge — the driver stalled after 8 attempts. **Fix:** every rightward hop's
   target now advances rightward (and leftward hops advance leftward).
3. **[Rule 1] Spike-beside-climb-entry strand (`F5→U1`).** A spike 52px before the U1
   mount made the spike hop sail past the mount's fire window. **Fix:** removed the
   F5 (and F4) climb-base spikes — a climb base is deliberately hazard-free (4 spikes).
4. **[Rule 1] Ceiling-bonk on the diamond mount (`T2→L`).** The L-road mount takeoff
   launched from under the H tier's underside and bonked it, stalling the climb. **Fix:**
   shifted L (and the upper spire) right so the mount clears H's right edge.
5. **[Rule 3] Terrain OBJECT_BUDGET overflow (715 > 650).** The tall two-spire design's
   wide tiers + long floors exceeded the browser-boot terrain object ceiling. **Fix:** a
   compaction pass — narrowed tiers to the ~70px-overlap minimum and shortened floors,
   landing at 566 objects.

Every one of these was invisible to the static suite and surfaced only by driving the
real engine — re-validating this project's "checks that don't play the game lie"
standard. Impact: geometry-only, scoped to `src/levels/level-02.js` + its
`smoke-progress.mjs` fixture. `audit-endgate-key.mjs` untouched.
