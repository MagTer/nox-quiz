The mandated mid-phase human sign-off: a real person plays the rebuilt prototype pair (level-01 calm swamp intro + level-02 intense/vertical swamp with the first math-skip key) and approves the shape BEFORE levels 03–08 are committed to it. This is rework insurance — the rebuild discards kid-validated geometry, so proving one calm + one intense archetype (and the redefined key mechanic) here de-risks Phase 38's kid-UAT.

This checkpoint also LOCKS two open design questions carried from RESEARCH:
- A1 — the full-XP value awarded on the key-skip clear (default 20 = XP_HARD; confirm or pick a different value).
- A3 — whether level-02's "intense + vertical" is satisfied by OPEN-AIR verticality (no ceilings, per §8's L1–2 rule, as built) or whether §8 should be amended to allow ceilings earlier.

Do NOT rubber-stamp this gate (standing project precedent, Phases 25/27/28) — even under auto_advance, a human must genuinely play and respond. The sign-off ideally involves the kid; at minimum the user.
