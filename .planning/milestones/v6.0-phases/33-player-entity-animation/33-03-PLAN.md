    Preserve the existing `if (player.getCurAnim()?.name !== target) { player.play(target); }` guard completely UNCHANGED — do not call `.play()` unconditionally, and do not remove or weaken this check.

    Do NOT hook the land trigger off `player.onGround()` (lines 50-53) — that hook fires on every grounded contact, including repeated hits while walking across adjacent floor colliders (this file's own comment already documents this exact behavior as the reason land SFX was removed from that hook). Reusing it here would make `landHold` keep re-triggering during ordinary walking, starving the `run` anim. The `wasFalling`-gated transition above only fires on a genuine airborne-to-grounded edge, avoiding that regression.
