<objective>
Prove the tappable-UI requirement (MOB-03) headlessly and wire the audio gesture gate (MOB-05 code). Tappable ANSWERS and MUTE fall out of the letterbox migration + touchToMouse for free (existing box.onClick targets) — but RESET does NOT: title.js's reset arm + Yes/No confirm are keyboard-only with no clickable widget, so touchToMouse has nothing to fire. This plan adds coarse-pointer-gated area()+onClick paths to the reset arm and Yes/No confirm (keyboard flow byte-identical), and PROVES answers+mute+reset-arm+confirm all resolve on a real Playwright touch tap — not assumed. And audio unlock must be wired to a genuine activation-triggering event (click/pointerup, never touchstart) with the iOS ITP eviction documented as an expectation. The real-device audio proof is deferred by design (headless synthetic taps grant activation).

Purpose: MOB-03 and MOB-05 are the "wire existing engine behavior through the unified mapping" requirements — plus the one gap where "existing" wasn't enough (reset was never clickable). This plan closes their buildable scope and draws the deferral line clearly for Phase 38's on-device gate.
