## Wave 0 Requirements

Existing infrastructure covers all phase behaviors — no new test framework or fixtures needed. The gates that prove this phase already exist in `scripts/`:

- `scripts/check-safety.sh` — proves the D-01 wrong-answer settle uses NO banned timer token (`setTimeout`/`setInterval`/`wait(`/`loop(`/`lifespan(`) and no punishment construct.
- `scripts/check-import-safety.sh` — proves new engine-global refs (`sprite`, `tween`, `loadSprite`) stay inside function bodies (a727c13).
- `scripts/check-assets-manifest.mjs` — proves every newly-declared sprite path (coin/key/goal) exists on disk.
- `scripts/validate-levels.mjs` — proves the goal (16px) and key (20px) collision footprints are unchanged after the sprite swaps (reachability preserved).
- `scripts/browser-boot.mjs` + `scripts/audit-phase21-mechanics.mjs` — prove goals still fire and keys still pick up across all levels in a real browser.
- `scripts/check-gate.sh` — proves the challenge/math-gate invariants survive the D-01 change.

## Manual-Only Verifications

These are automation-blind (crispness, "reads-as" legibility, feel) and REQUIRE human/interactive proof — per CLAUDE.md's verification standard and RESEARCH.md's Validation Architecture. A `checkpoint:human-verify` covers the CC0 licensing check too.

| Behavior | Requirement | Why Manual | Test Instructions |
|----------|-------------|------------|-------------------|
| Wrong answer visibly registers; mashing 1‑2‑3‑4 no longer slips a wrong pick past | polish (D-01) | "feels wrong / registers" and "mash defeated" are perceptual, not assertable by a gate | Serve the game, open a math challenge (door/gate/enemy/end-gate), rapidly mash all four keys — confirm a wrong pick flashes/shakes and input is inert during the brief settle, and you cannot blow through it; a correct pick still clears instantly. Confirm it never feels punishing (no lost progress, no game-over). |
| Coin reads as crisp, not low-res blocky | polish (D-02) | Image crispness is visual | Serve the game, walk over coins on a level, eyeball the sprite at the 1.5× display scale — confirm it looks crisp and on-palette (gold, no pink) and the spin animates. |
| Goal reads unmistakably as a finish flag (not a key) | polish (D-03) | "reads as a flag" is perceptual legibility | Serve the game, reach the goal on ≥2 levels — confirm it reads as a checkered finish flag and the goal still fires (LEVEL CLEAR). |
| Key pickup reads as a key AND its "skip the final math" purpose is legible | polish (D-04) | "reads as a key" + framing clarity is perceptual | On an even level (02/04/06/08), grab the key — confirm the pickup looks like a key (not a box), the popup/HUD wording makes the skip purpose clear, and holding it skips the end math for full XP. |
| CC0 assets are genuinely CC0 (not CC-BY mislabeled) | polish (D-02/03/04) | License provenance must be human-checked (RESEARCH caught a CC-BY asset surfacing as CC0) | Before vendoring, open each asset's source page, confirm the License field is CC0 / public-domain, and record it in `assets/LICENSES/` + `CREDITS.md`. |
