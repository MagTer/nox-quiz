<objective>
Replace Phase 18's procedurally-generated parallax layers (`assets/parallax/{far,mid,near}.png`)
and title/select backdrop (`assets/tiles/title-bg.png`) with real, curated, composited CC0
pixel-art scenery from Kenney's "Background Elements" pack, through Phase 18's unchanged
technical contract — no src/ code changes. Extends the same `scripts/build-art-assets.py`
pipeline Plan 01 created (hence depends on it and shares its wave-ordering file).

Purpose: ART-07 and ART-08 both fail today because `scripts/generate-art-assets.py` draws random
rectangles as a "skyline" and low-contrast noise panels as a "backdrop" — neither depicts real
composed scenery. This plan composites real silhouette elements (temple/castle/tower/hills/
mountains) from Kenney's purpose-built background kit into the locked frame geometries, and
records matching CC0 license proof.

Output: `scripts/build-art-assets.py` extended with `build_parallax()`/`build_title_bg()`,
`assets/parallax/{far,mid,near}.png` + `assets/tiles/title-bg.png` (real composited content,
unchanged dimensions), new `assets/LICENSES/parallax.txt` + `title-bg.txt`, updated `CREDITS.md`
rows.
</objective>
