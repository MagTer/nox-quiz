asset swap, and — critically — obtain a genuine, blocking, recorded human visual sign-off before

final task is designed to be robust against that default, not rely on it.

Output: `scripts/screenshot-phase20.mjs`, 5 real screenshots of the running game with the new
art, a full green automated suite re-run, and `20-VERIFICATION.md` recording a literal,
non-fabricated human response.
