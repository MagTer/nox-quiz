<objective>
Make finding a secret alcove actually feel like finding a secret (MECH-03), and give the
level-select screen a positive-only record of which levels she's found one on (MECH-06),
through the guarded, version-bumped save seam.

Purpose: the secret alcoves have existed since Phase 25 but are silent and invisible —
nothing happens on touch beyond a quiet XP bump, and nothing on the select screen ever
shows she found one. This plan closes that gap with reused assets (existing `fx.pop()`,
existing `"pickup"` SFX vacated by Plan 29-01's collect.js deletion) plus one small new
world-space popup effect and one small new select-screen marker — no new asset sourcing
(that's Phase 31+).
Output: `src/progress.js` gains `markSecretFound`/`hasSecretFound`; `CONFIG.SAVE.VERSION`
bumps 2→3 (deliberate, no-migration reset, per project convention); `src/fx.js` gains a
`popupText` effect; `src/mechanics/secretAlcove.js` fires burst+chime+popup+persisted-mark
on touch; `src/scenes/select.js` renders a star marker on levels with a found secret.
</objective>
