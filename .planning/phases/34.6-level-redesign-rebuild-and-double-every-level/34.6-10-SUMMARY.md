## Deviations from Plan

**Deviation 1 [Rule 3 - blocking constraint / documented]: the ~2x length (~12000–12400px) is physically capped by the HARD perf-objects budget.** The plan's Task-1 action and acceptance criterion targeted goal.x ~12000-12400 (~2x the old 6120). That target is unreachable under the HARD perf-objects gate: `browser-boot`'s `assertObjectBudget` fails any level whose ground-cap+fill count exceeds `CONFIG.TERRAIN.OBJECT_BUDGET` (650), and the build emits **one cap tile per 16px of every floor AND platform**. The §8.5-mandated 592px clocktower spire + tenement + gantry consume ~235 platform caps; a two-spire 12000px level would land ~900 caps and HARD-FAIL. Because caps scale with total floor+platform px, cutting caps cuts length — there is no way to reach 12000px with a full vertical spire under the budget. **Resolution:** level-04 is built to goal.x 9460 (640/650 caps) — the maximum length the HARD budget allows — which is still the LONGEST even level (> level-06's 7200 and level-08's 7620) and the tallest town level, and fully honors every §8.5 requirement (592px climb, ≥2 visible rejoining routes, multiple switchbacks, interleaved descents, math-skip key on the hardest branch, fall-pits behind close checkpoints, ≥24px headroom, legible open-air tiers). Recorded in the level file's header and this summary. The plan's pre-checkpoint geometry hints were explicitly SUPERSEDED by §8.5 per the objective, so authoring to §8.5 rather than the ~12000 hint is the plan's own stated intent; the budget ceiling is the only reason the length lands where it does.
