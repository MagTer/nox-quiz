## Accomplishments
- Rebuilt `src/levels/level-05.js` entirely (append-only suspended, §9.1): 9 graveyard runs, 15 platforms (all `h:16`, all off-grid `y`), 35 coins, 2 spikes, 1 door, 1 enemy, `mathGates: []`, 1 secret alcove, 16 checkpoints, explicit `bounds` with `right` hand-bumped to 7020.
- Authored the graveyard's distinct signature: a MANDATORY four-tier MAUSOLEUM climb (RA->RB->RC->RD peak y:60 = 260px -> RE descent) over a 700px sunken crypt pit, made mandatory because both the descent tier RE (gap 230 > 162 reach from RC) and the far floor F3 are beyond a bare running jump — so the up-and-over is the only route across (validator jump-envelope BFS confirms).
- Kept the mandatory rises a calm 58-70px (the §8 non-overlapping 60-70 band) while still reaching the odd-band top (~260px) by using four gentle ascent tiers rather than three steeper ones.
- Put the game's cemetery overlapping-tier ceilings on an OPTIONAL route only (the crypt vault VT1/VT2/VT3 at rise 74 -> 26px headroom, the belltower tech from 34.6-05), keeping the required graveyard path open/legible (§8.5 rule 6).
- Added a visible low/high grave-mound fork (GM1/GM2/GM3, 3 bonus coins) and a tombstone row (SS1/SS2/SS3) for beat-to-beat variety, with the mausoleum as the vertical centerpiece.
- Kept it a calm-but-harder-than-03 intro: 140-160px cemetery-band gaps, only 2 spikes (each ≥250px from both floor edges, clear of every platform footprint), and dense checkpoints (one per mausoleum tier) so any crypt-pit fall costs seconds.
- No smoke-progress golden fixture exists for level-05 (only 01-04 do); confirmed the suite still PASSes without adding one.

## Decisions Made
- Reused the proven 34.6-02/05 calm-intro skeleton (mandatory stepped climb over a wide pit + low/high fork + optional overlapping-tier climb) as level-05's mausoleum + crypt-vault template rather than inventing new climb physics — the risk was in the graveyard character and reaching a taller peak, not the arch.
- Made the mausoleum genuinely taller (260px, 4 ascent tiers) rather than matching level-03's 200px 3-tier rooftop, so level-05 reads as a real step up in verticality while keeping every individual rise inside the calm 58-70px band.
- Put ALL overlapping-tier ceilings on the OPTIONAL crypt vault so the required graveyard path never forces a cramped duck — the calm way to introduce cemetery ceilings.
- Only 2 spikes (vs level-03's 4): the sunken crypt pit is the level's main fall hazard, and over-spiking the calm intro would work against the §8.5-rule-5 "most-forgiving ramp" intent for the early biomes.

## Deviations from Plan
None - the level validated, drove, and audited clean on the first authored geometry (no in-engine defects surfaced; the ≥250px spike-margin and mandatory-peak-via-out-of-reach-far-floor patterns inherited from 34.6-02/05 were applied from the start rather than rediscovered). The PLAN's pre-checkpoint geometry hints (goal ~6600-6800, L3-6 140-160 gaps) were honored; the §8.5 raised bar drove the actual shape as instructed.
