<objective>
Extend the interactive mechanic audit so it RIDES every moving platform and CROSSES every patroller — the in-engine proof the phase's verification standard demands ("checks that don't play the game lie"). Today `deriveEncounters()` (mechanic-drive.mjs) emits door/math-gate/enemy/secret-alcove encounters and the audit drives to each; it is blind to movers and patrollers. Add both, following the Phase-30 alcove precedent (the model for adding a NEW encounter kind with its own drive-and-detect signal), and prove it RED-first against a fixture.

Purpose: The SC5 human sign-off and the consolidation gate both rely on this audit reporting that every mover was actually ridden and every patroller actually respawned her. Without the extension a placed mover ships with zero interactive proof — exactly the gap the standard forbids.

Output: `deriveEncounters` mover/patroller cases; a ride-detector (player carried N grounded frames while on the platform) and a cross-detector (contact fired `respawn()` — player pos snapped to `lastCheckpoint`); the audit's pass gate folds both in; a GREEN companion fixture for the RED-first proof.
</objective>
