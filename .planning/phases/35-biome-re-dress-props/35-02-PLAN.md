<objective>
Prove the whole props pipeline end-to-end on TWO trial levels of TWO different biomes at different intensities — the locked prototype-first pattern. Bake the swamp + cemetery prop sprites from the already-vendored, style-board-approved Gothicvania packs, wire them through the manifest, and hand-place a RESTRAINED props array on level-01 (swamp, calm, THIN vocabulary — exercises the sourcing/bake pipeline) and level-06 (cemetery, vertical switchback, RICH vocabulary — exercises density-vs-legibility + climb screenshots). Trial pair (confirmed per RESEARCH A5): **level-01 (swamp)** + **level-06 (cemetery)**.

HARD constraints threaded here:
- Geometry BYTE-FROZEN: touch ONLY the new top-level `props` key on each descriptor; nothing inside `geometry`. check-geometry-frozen.mjs must stay green.
- RESTRAINED / legibility-first: props NEVER obscure a platform, route, hazard, coin, key, or mechanic. Sparse atmospheric accents only. Background-depth (Z_BACK) or on-surface (Z_SURFACE) — NO foreground over the play lane.
- Prop art discipline (ART-PARITY-STEERING, normative): name EXACT source paths + crop rects + retint tuples; native color only; NEVER `_remap_luminance()`; NEVER scale-to-cell; retint only via `hue_shift_band`; use PRE-SLICED files, never a preview sheet.
</objective>
